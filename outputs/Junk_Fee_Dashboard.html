Chicago Neighborhoods: Estimated Fee Burden

Junk Fee Burden by Community Area

Each circle is a Chicago community area. Circle size = number of renter households (ACS 2022). Circle color = estimated annual junk fee burden per household, based on median rent (RentCafe 2026 where available, ACS 2022 otherwise) and corporate landlord presence. Darker red = higher burden. Click any circle for full details and data source.

Methodology (no interpolation): Chicago does not collect neighborhood-level junk fee data. This is a key gap that PRO Component 02 (Rental Registry) would fill.

What the estimate uses:
1. Median rent by community area: RentCafe/Yardi Matrix (March 2026, buildings 50+ units) for 14 neighborhoods with direct matches. ACS 5-Year 2022 (Table B25064) for remaining areas.
2. Fee burden rate: Urban Institute (2024) documents 10-30% of base rent at large corporate-managed properties. We apply 25% for High corporate-landlord areas, 18% for Medium, 12% for Low.
3. Corporate landlord classification: Based on CMAP land use data on Class A/B multifamily density. "High" = significant professionally managed multifamily stock. "Low" = predominantly small landlords and 2-4 flats.
4. Annual burden = monthly rent x fee rate x 12. This is a range estimate, not observed data.

Sources: RentCafe/Yardi Matrix (Mar 2026), ACS 5-Year 2022, Urban Institute (2024), CMAP land use data.
